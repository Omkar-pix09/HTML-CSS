About Our Village

Panhala is a historic hill station and fort city located in Maharashtra, India. Known for its pleasant climate and rich heritage, Panhala played a major role in the history of the Maratha Empire, especially under the leadership of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The majestic Panhala Fort, scenic landscapes, and ancient monuments make it a popular destination for history lovers and travelers alike.
